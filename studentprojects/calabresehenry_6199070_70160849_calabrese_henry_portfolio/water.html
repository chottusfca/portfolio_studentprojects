"Clear Water"

Date Completed: Fall 2021
Class: Design Fundamentals




Description

"Clear Water" is the title of the above compositional grid, made as an interpretation of USF's own Lo Schiavo building. When thinking about the building, I think about the sleak and modern design as well as the sense of calmness the surrounding space brings me. Attempting to recreate these styles and emotions in my grid, I utilized shades of blues and yellows while illustrating various corners of the building in a balanced and hierarchical way.
