"Toast"

Date Completed: Fall 2021
Class: Design Fundamentals




Description

"Toast" was my first ever design project. The task was to create something of a product brochure for an item in our home. Choosing my toaster oven, I wanted my brochure to show off its useful features in a clear and concise way. The color pallette is heavy on browns and greys, so as to mimic the colors of toast and the oven itself. The inside spread shows off both the toaster's function as well as its endless possibilities, advertising it as a truly great investment.
