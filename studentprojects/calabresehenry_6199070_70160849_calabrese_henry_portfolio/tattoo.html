Tattoo You

Date Completed: Spring 2022
Class: Design Fundamentals




Description

Tattoo You marks my first completed project in Visual Communications 1. The assignment was to design ourselves a tattoo, carrying with it personal meaning and significance. This design's composition is composed of a set of iconography, each representing a different member of my family. The tattoo's simplistic design of black and white linework is indicative of my own style, as if I were to get a tattoo it would be in this tone.
