Tufts

The Tufts University AS&E Graduate Student Council is an official representative body at Tufts University for students in the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences. This logo was the official winner of a contest held during a rebranding period. It now serves as the official logo of all things related to the council. The logo itself gave a fresh new spin on the elephant mascot of Tufts University. While most of their icons and logos display the elephant in a serious manner, this logo portrayed a fun sense of life that allowed an obvious break from the redundancy. It’s composed of five separate shapes positioned intentionally to allow the white space to create the rest of the image for the viewer. If you look closely at each shape, you’ll notice there is a subtle “GSC” spelt out from the top shape forming the elephant's trunk, moving diagonally down to the bottom shape forming the bottom of the elephant's body.
