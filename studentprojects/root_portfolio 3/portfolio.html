"Beauty Killer"

An anti- war poster created during the start of the war in Ukraine, early 2022. The intent behind it was to make Ukraine an idealistic, fantasy land to emphasize their flourishing country before destruction hit. The stylistic inspiration behind this piece was the Wizard of Oz, which connected to the sense of a utopian society. The red tank was decorated with glitter to represent the red slippers in the movie, symbolic of revolution. Although the slippers' powerful forces are in the wrong hands, which is why destruction is being shown. Setting this piece in Ukraines' sunflowers fields, also hinting at their flag with the surrealist blue background.

"Type Compositions"

The featured type studies were developed in Fall of 2022. The content and compositions contend with a diversity of subject matters, mediums, typographic arrangements, hierarchy of information, and underlying text/grid structures. This series serves to catalogue a foundational understanding of how text and composition come together to create meaning and interest. These type studies also establish an understanding of typographic experimentation, functionality, and working within visual constraints/systems.

"Layer of Sounds"

Given the task to make three icons that represented San Francisco. Developed in Spring 2022. Raised in the Bay Area, something that Ava uses often for traveling back home is different modes of transportation. Most muni and bart, cycling on quiet days.The goal was to represent their sounds by layering each icon matching them with color schemes that not only create synergy but also capture their ethos. Sound is a huge part of San Franciscos' identity, working with opacities helps to visualize how sound lingers throughout the city.

"Principles of Design"

One of the first pieces Ava made as a design student in the Fall of 2021. It set off her admiration for editorial design. The task was to photograph five different design principles and create an infographic for each one. Choosing a layout to imitate a fun, youthful magazine cover, achieved by bold font choices and colors. As well as the positioning of the layout to allow the photos to communicate the main ideas as if it were a cover page.

"SF Foghorn: Editorial Designs"

These are the first issues that were printed for the Foghorn newspaper, starting from Fall 2022 to present day. For the scene and opinion sections of the paper. Given the task to decorate the page for the scene, and stick to a specific traditional layout for opinion. Articles included are both scene pieces. One aims to enlighten students on the best spots to study, while the other investigates old and forgotten archives in the library. Aiming to create a sense of hierarchy to each story, as well make each story visually pleasing to attract readers.

"Tattoo You"

Asked to create a tattoo based on something in your life, important in your life, etc... Ava chose her dog Sadie who passed a couple months prior, winter 2021. She chose to integrate water, and Lake Tahoe. Since that was her dog's favorite spot. The first, shows Sadie shaking water off while displaying sun beams. Sadie was her golden girl. The other shows Sadie looking off at the Lake, a calm setting to represent that she is at peace.
