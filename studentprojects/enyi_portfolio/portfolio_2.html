One with Golden Park

Course: Design Fundamentals – Fall 2022 – Poster Series

The first icon shows how children interact with nature. Using their eyes, they could visually see the flower's beauty. With their noses, they could enjoy the smell of the floral scent coming from the flower

The second icon represents how nature has boundaries and one should keep their distance so both can still entertain one another.

The last icon shows a common activity of done at the Golden Gate Park which is birdwatching. People would set up their chairs and use their binoculars to admire the endless sky and watch the flying creates roam the sky.

Description

San Francisco has many different colorful history shaped by many people. “One with Golden Park” are three cohesive icons each expressing an activity people can do when visiting Golden Gate park. This piece communicates to people the consistency of both nature and humans.The main message shown is sometimes we forget that interacting with nature can also be a source of entertainment other than just staying home and going onto your laptop. One can take a lot of inspiration from the environment one life in.
