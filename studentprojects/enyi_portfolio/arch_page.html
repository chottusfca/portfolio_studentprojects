USF Color Architecture

Design Fundamentals -- Fall 2021

Architectural Shapes and Color

The objective of this assignment is to take nine architectural pictures of USF and fill the spaces with only five colors. The twist is to create a sense of form only using these five colors. After taking many different photos, we had to find the ones that had the most dimension in them. This project made me learn that you need to plan things ahead of time because, without strategic planning, you may not be able to grasp that specific goal.
