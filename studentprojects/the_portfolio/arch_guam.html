Guam Hospital Development

International Projects ARCH 340-02

Professor Seth Wachtel

Spring 2022

The Guam Hospital Development project consisted of an extenstion to the existing hospital in the Guam Regional Medical City as well as other amenities and site components. The team divided this project into multiple phases, from design sketches to final renders. Using the vast culture and history of Guam, team members incorporated native plants, latte stones, and plenty of natural light and ventilation throughout the site. Adding on to the hospital extension is a courtyard connected to a cafeteria/cafe through trellises, office spaces connecting to a parking garage, and a small ampitheater space in the northeast corner.
