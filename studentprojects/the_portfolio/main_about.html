Unubold Munkhbold is an architect/designer located in the San Francisco Bay Area. As a first-generation immigrant and college student, Unubold has walked a unique path. Now a 4th-year at the University of San Francisco with plans to graduate in the spring of 2023, he is taking on Architecture as his main path, with Design and Asian Studies also under his wing.

His love for design and creativity has fueled his passion for architecture, leading to a multidisciplinary work ethic and worldview. A native of Mongolia and the Bay Area, Unubold uses both the culture he comes from and the culture he has grown up with to design with purpose and progress.
