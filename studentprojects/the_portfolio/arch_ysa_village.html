YSA Village

International Projects ARCH 340-02

Professor Seth Wachtel

Spring 2022

The Youth Spirit Artworks (YSA) Village is a multi-phase project consisting of layout, design, materialtiy, and construction. A year and a half ago, a tiny house village designed by students in the same course opened in Oakland. It now houses two dozen formerly homeless youth between 18-25. Building on previous classesâ€™ contribution to this project, the team used precedents and the existing village to design another village to be built adjacent to the first. This small community is complete with parking, a recreation center, community kitchen and dining area, gardens, and semi-private bathrooms. The primary focus with the second village is to scale the process so that we can demonstrate how to shelter hundreds, even thousands of people in need. This will be a demonstration site that shows a clear vision as well as providing housing.
