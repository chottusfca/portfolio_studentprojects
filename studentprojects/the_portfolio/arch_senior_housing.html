Affordable Senior Housing

Studio 3 ARCH 230-01

Professor Jeff Oberdorfer

Spring 2021

As the last project in the Housing Studio, the Lot 7 Senior Affordable Housing project focused on designing for the elderly in combination with ADA regulations. The finished product is a 6-story structure with single and double bedroom units, recreational spaces, outdoor spaces and balconies for each unit, a living roof with additional recreational space, and ample parking spaces.
