My name is Alvin Chan and I am currently a senior at the
University of San Francisco with plans to graduate in Fall of 2023.
I am a design major with a minor in environmental science.
I have been focusing my attention on
graphic design and 3D modeling. As a graphic designer and modeler
I thrive to create meaning
and a story behind each piece. I depend on
the process of sustainability and versatility in my work.
I have a background in almost all adobe applications
from Photoshop to Premiere Pro. Animation and creating art in a 3D space
has always been a passion of mine
and I hope to integrate these aspects into a professional career.
The role of a designer is a tough role to fill,
but I am hungry to start this challenge.
