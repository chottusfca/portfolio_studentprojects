NEPTUNE TAPES:
VOLUME 1 2022

This piece was inspired by old 1990s Japanese infographics and magazines.
The 90s style anime look was one of the key features I was trying to push.
Adding new kinds of shading like the black and white manga texture adds
the realism of japanese magazines.

FLOWER HILL 2020

Studio Ghibli has been one of the most popular animation studios for decades.
Films like Spirited Away and My Neighbor Totoro, have gained a cult-like following
in the US. This piece is a replication of the beautiful scene in My Neighbor Totoro.
The piece was done in a blender and with a custom texture, and fully animated.

SPACE INVADERS 2021

Space Invaders was created for the soul purpose of learning basic gemometric
models and understanding the fundamentals of vehicle movement. I modeled
the spaceships, environment, and animated in Blender

CAR RIDE 2022

Car Ride uses 90s anime aesthetic to portray a late night drive in Japan.
I got inspired by a lot of 1990s seinen animes.

JAPAN STORES 2022

These stores are a combination of flower shops to cellphone shops
in Japan. I modeled all of these stores using a cominbation
of curves, basic geometric shape, particle effects, and a variant
of toon/anime shading.

PONYO 2020

Ponyo is a Studio Ghibili movie based on a fish who became a girl through friendship
and family love. This was a childhood movie of mine, so I wanted to create a
3D version of Ponyo.
